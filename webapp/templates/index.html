Our Mandate

The State Remote Sensing Application Centre (SRSAC), Department of Science & Technology, Government of Rajasthan, is dedicated to the establishment of ground stations for receiving satellite data, generation of value-added data products, and the application of remote sensing and GIS technologies for state development and governance.
Since 1985, SRSAC has collaborated with ISRO and multiple departments to provide geospatial support for agriculture, forestry, water resources, watershed, mining, urban/rural planning, disaster management, and more.
Vision: To move from information support towards decision support for sustainable development and management of natural resources in Rajasthan, leveraging state-of-the-art remote sensing and GIS.
